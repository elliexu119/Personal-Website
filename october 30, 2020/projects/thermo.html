What is it

A thermoelectric based container that uses the Peltier-Seebeck effect to function as a fridge, oven, and power generator

Using the principles of thermodynamics, I designed power circuits that could turn a container in a fridge, oven, and power generator at the touch of a switch.

Applying temperature sensors and other electrical components, it knows when it should cool or heat its contents.

A modified H-bridge circuit allows the container to generate electricity over a temperature gradient.

This function can be activated over Wifi on any device as the container has an IP address assigned to it.
