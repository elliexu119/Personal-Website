An encoder/decoder that physically writes out the messages.

Mechanical

A CNC system that uses a pen to write out the message on a sheet of paper inserted on the bottom.

Software

Reads a user inputted message, then the user is able to select the encoder or the decoder to process the texts.

Collaboration

Worked together with a team of 3. My main focus was design of the system (i.e. how everything comes together), the mechanical design, and the contruction of the writer.
