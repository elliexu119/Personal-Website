An Interactive Dog Toy

A solution to lazy dog-lovers who want a pet - without having to walk or run the dog.

Good Doggo is a ball-shaped runaway toy that uses a flashing, colour-changing LED and the allure of dog food to incite your pet into chasing it. Once, the dog catches it, dog food will fall out allowing the dog access to its delicious reward.

Used SolidWorks to plan possible configurations. Used an Arduino as the microcontroller along with multiple sensors such as an accelerometer and an ultrasonic sensor.
