A multifunctional twitter analyzer

Using Python, it web scrapes tweets from Twitter and can perfom:

· Sentiment analysis for a topic such as “coronavirus” over a period of time – considers factors such as # of retweets, subject, etc.

· Hottest word in a time frame (for the last month of April, it has been “Trump” and “ratings”).

Companion app to the Tulip & Carnation stock analysis programs.
